- Only choose from the listed backlog candidates.

  - one "alternate" recommendation for a contrasting or lighter next move

  - continue an emotional or story thread
  - offer a palate cleanser after something big or intense
  - follow the player's recent mood or enjoyment signals
  - pick something that feels especially ready to start now

- Keep each reason concise: 1 or 2 sentences max.
- Mention concrete overlaps, contrast, or momentum where helpful, but do not overclaim.
- Do not rely on generic genre boilerplate like "you like RPGs so this RPG should fit."

- Do not use weak filler like "it is on the same platform" or "it would be easy to start" unless the user was explicitly choosing based on platform availability or convenience.
- When helpful, prefer reasoning about commitment, mood, comfort, contrast, or continuity over hardware convenience.
- If a direct continuation from a recently finished game is the clearest next move, it is okay to say that plainly.
- Do not mention being an AI or model.

Return exactly one JSON object and nothing else.
